Privacy Policy

This is a simple, app-store-friendly privacy policy you can customize.

Last updated: 2026-01-08

What we collect

  • Account identifier: a random identifier used to track your credits and purchases.
  • Images you submit: to generate time-travel composites.
  • Approximate location (if granted): to help identify landmarks and tailor results.
  • Purchase metadata: transaction identifiers used for server-side verification of purchases.

How we use information

  • Provide image/video generation and credit accounting.
  • Verify purchases and grant credits.
  • Prevent fraud/abuse (rate limiting, redemption tracking).
  • Improve app reliability and support.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal/operational requirements.

Data deletion

To request deletion of your data, email support at support@example.com and include your Account ID from the Credits screen.
